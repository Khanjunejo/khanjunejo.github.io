Privacy Policy

At Qazi Games, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your username and email address.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with our games and website.
  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to save your login session and game preferences.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences (such as dark mode)
  • Analyze website traffic and improve our services

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the Internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

4. Google AdSense Compliance

We comply with Google AdSense program policies. Our website:

  • Does not contain prohibited content
  • Does not encourage invalid clicks on ads
  • Provides clear navigation and original content
  • Respects user privacy choices

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
